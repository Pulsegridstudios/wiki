## Overview

The **Systems** section contains technical reference pages for the major systems used throughout the Looped Operations facility.

This section is intended to explain how plant systems function, how they interact, and what role they play in safe and stable reactor operation.

You will typically find:

- cooling and circulation system reference
- feedwater and steam system pages
- electrical and distribution system explanations
- shutdown, protection, and support system overviews
- technical descriptions of plant interactions

---

## What this section covers

The Systems section is designed to help operators and contributors understand:

- what each major plant system does
- how systems affect one another
- how failures or changes in one area can influence the plant as a whole
- which system readings are important during operation
