## Overview

The **Electrical System** distributes power throughout the plant and exports generated electricity to the grid.

---

## What it does

- distributes internal plant power  
- supports critical systems  
- exports electricity to external systems  

---

## Key Components

- Generators  
- Transformers  
- Switchgear  
- Breakers  
- Backup Power Systems  

---

## Why it matters

> ⚠️ **Warning**  
> Loss of electrical supply can disable critical safety systems.
