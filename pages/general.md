## Overview

The **General** section contains core reference material for understanding the Looped Operations facility, its reactor type, and the systems operators interact with on a day-to-day basis.

This section is intended to provide foundational knowledge before moving on to detailed operating procedures or emergency response guides.

You will typically find:

- reactor background and concepts
- control room orientation
- system reference information
- operator-facing explanations of plant behaviour

---

## What this section covers

The General section is designed to help operators and readers understand:

- what kind of reactor the facility is based on
- how major systems relate to one another
- how to read and interpret control room information
- the core terminology used throughout the wiki
