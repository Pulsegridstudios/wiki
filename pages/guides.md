## Overview

The **Guides** section contains step-by-step operating procedures for running systems within the Looped Operations facility.

This section is intended to support normal plant operation by providing clear, structured instructions for startup, shutdown, monitoring, and routine workflows.

You will typically find:

- startup and shutdown procedures
- normal operating workflows
- step-by-step control room actions
- practical operator guidance for standard plant conditions

---

## What this section covers

The Guides section is designed to help operators:

- follow safe and consistent operating procedures
- understand the correct order of actions
- verify plant response during normal operation
- maintain stable and efficient system conditions
