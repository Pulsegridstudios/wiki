## Overview

This page explains how the Looped Operations Wiki uses cookies, local storage, and similar browser-based features.

The wiki is designed to keep data collection minimal and only use browser storage where needed for core functionality and user preferences.

---

## Cookies and Local Storage

This site may use **essential cookies** and **local storage** for basic functionality.

These may include:

- remembering theme preference
- remembering interface settings
- remembering whether cookie notices have been dismissed
- storing other basic site preferences

These features help the wiki function correctly and improve usability.

---

## What We Store

The wiki may store small pieces of information in your browser, such as:

- theme choice (light or dark mode)
- whether the cookie banner has been accepted or dismissed
- other site preference values used for interface behaviour

No advertising or tracking cookies are intentionally used by the wiki itself.

---

## Why We Use These Features

We use browser storage to:

- improve usability
- preserve your preferences between page visits
- support essential site features
- reduce repeated prompts such as cookie notices

---

## Third-Party Services

If third-party services are added in future, such as embedded media, analytics, or external widgets, those services may use their own cookies or tracking technologies.

Any such usage should be reviewed separately and may be added to this notice if required.

---

## Your Choices

You can control or clear cookies and local storage through your browser settings.

Please note that disabling all storage features may affect certain site functions, including saved preferences.
